**Verbose mode** forces full field extraction on every event, even when transforming commands (`stats`, `chart`, `timechart`) are present. It always generates the Events tab alongside Statistics and Visualizations. This gives you the most complete view of your data — but at the cost of speed and system resources. Avoid Verbose mode on large datasets or in production dashboards.
