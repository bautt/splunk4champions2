### Highlighting TERMS: 

The easiest way to see minor and major breakers in action is to move the mouse over the log terms: 
whatever you can highlight in one peace contains only minor breakers and can be used with TERM or PREFIX directives:

<SplunkSearch earliest="-1h" latest="now" spl={`index=_internal`}></SplunkSearch>

Open the search above and move the mouse slowly over the result TERMs:
